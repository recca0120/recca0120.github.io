VMware Fusion Player offers a free personal license, though the application page occasionally shows up blank -- just refresh a few times.

## How to Apply

Go to the [VMware Fusion Player Personal Use page](https://customerconnect.vmware.com/evalcenter?p=fusion-player-personal-13), click Create an Account, and once registered you'll find the License Key and download link in the License & Download section below.

## Registration Info

If form validation fails during registration, try the following details:

- Email address: use your real email
- City: Sacramento
- Zip or postal code: 94203
- Country/Territory: United States
- State or province: California
- Business phone: 0013602923672
